File Hills Collection

Historic photographs and belongings from the Smithsonian’s National Museum of the American Indian (NMAI), documenting life, dress, ceremony, and community in Saskatchewan from the late 1800s to the mid-1900s. Some culturally sensitive items are described but not displayed out of respect.

Explore the Collection Courtesy of the Smithsonian’s National Museum of the American Indian.
